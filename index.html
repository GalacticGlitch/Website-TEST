Nether Mobs

The Nether might be the most dangerous dimension of them all because of the mobs located there, so here is the list:

1. Blazes

Blazes are the only type of mob that are damaged by snowballs

Drops: Blaze rods

2. Wither Skeletons

3. Piglins/Baby Piglins

Wear golden armour and the normal Piglins won't attack you. They LOVE gold and if you give them some they will give you things in return, this is called bartering.

Drops: Golden Swords or Crossbows (depends on what their holding)

3.5. Piglin Brutes

Even if you wear golden armor Piglin Brutes will still attack you, and they do not barter.

Drops: Golden Axe

4. Hoglins/Baby Hoglins

5. Ghasts

6. Striders

You can ride Striders with saddles and a Warped Fungus on a Stick. (They are the only non-hostile mob in the nether).

They let you go across lava without touching it, but it does NOT make you fire resistant.

Drops: String
